The aforementioned are just a sample among the multitude of places where slang is used to measure your street wise intellect. Some people myself included always feel left out when we are not aware of the meaning of a new slang phrase/term/word. Being aware of the slang used in a particular area helps one to follow discussions and engage meaningfully.

So if you need to be aware of the new slang phrase/term/word, who do you consult? , where can you get the meanings instantly?, how do you pronounce the words?, how do you know if the words are nouns, verbs or adjectives?

Zimbabwean Slang Dictionary(ZIMSLANG) was developed to address all the questions raised above. The website consists of an A to Z index with each letter having correspondind entries (words) that begin with that letter. It is just be a replica of a normal dictionary but the only difference is that it will contain slang terms which are unique to Zimbabwe. The website is responsive to any device you access it from and very user friendly . The website contains forms which allow you to request or add words to the dictionary since slang is constantly changing and evolving for the benefit of everyone who is as curious as you and me.
